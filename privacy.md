Privacy Policy for ModelPrompt
Last Updated: December 31, 2024
Developer: Almara AI

Overview
ModelPrompt is a prompt optimization tool that helps users create better prompts for AI image and video generation models. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Data Collection
We do not collect any personal data.
ModelPrompt:

Does NOT require account creation or login
Does NOT collect your email, name, or contact information
Does NOT track your location
Does NOT collect or store the prompts you create
Does NOT use analytics or tracking software
Does NOT share any data with third parties


How the App Works
All prompt generation happens locally on your device. Your prompts, character presets, and preferences are stored only on your device and are never transmitted to our servers.

In-App Purchases
If you choose to purchase ModelPrompt Pro, the transaction is processed entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store). We do not have access to your payment information. These transactions are subject to Apple's Privacy Policy or Google's Privacy Policy.

Third-Party Services
The app does not connect to any third-party services except for processing in-app purchases through the respective app stores.

Children's Privacy
ModelPrompt does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Policy
